United States of Secrets: Edward Snowden Part 2

United States Scandal Part 2:

  • The world now know about “The Program”.

  • Edward Snowden use to works with the NSA.

  • Edward Snowden meet with Glenn Greedwald of theGuardain and documentary filmmaker Laura Poitras in Mira hotel, Hong Kong 2018.

  • Edward leak a lot of documents about “The Program”.

  • The first major document is about Verizon giving all their phone record to NSA.

  • General Clapper denied that NSA spy on its citizens in court a month before the document was exposed.

  • The story of the program was published by theGuardain, but this time they have the evidence to back up their claim.

  • The second major document was Prism: This document show 9 big internet companies cooperating with NSA since the beginning of 2007. (Microsoft, Yahoo, Google, Facebook, PalTalk, YouTube, Skype, AOL, and Apple)

  • The Washington Post on the second major document.

  • This was outrageous because the public finally found out that NSA was noting collecting data on phone call only, but everything (pictures, video, anything a person post online).

  • Obama told the press that NSA wasn’t looking at people telephone calls and contents.

  • After this incident, Snowden finally reveal his identify as the whistleblower.

  • Creating a video that expose “The Program” (Known to the public as Prism) with all the document he have that supported his claims.

  • He claims that people privacy should be depend on the people not the government officials.

  • Russia grant Edward Snowden Asylum.

  • Third major document is about a program named Muscular: used to spy/collect data oversea whenever and wherever they want.

  • Splitter is the physical connection that NSA used to copy internet traffic.

  • Conclusion: We can’t hold our government responsible because they are doing this for a reason (to prevent future terrorist attack). We can’t have one hundred percent security with a hundred percent privacy.
