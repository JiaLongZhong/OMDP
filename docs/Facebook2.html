Facebook Dilemma Part 2

The Fall of Facebook:

  • • 62 percent of people get their news from Facebook. Becoming one of the main distributors of news.

  • • 2016 Election was hugely impact by the media.

  • • Donald Trump digital media director, Brad Parscale took advantage of Facebook power advertising tools.

  • • Brad is searching for all the Trump Supporter and people who are likely to vote.

  • • He want to target them with Trump ads, telling them to vote for Trump.

  • • Facebook doesn’t take responsibilities of fake news. Causing huge misleading contexts/information to spread rapidly.

  • • Fake news became more interesting since it creative, and real news became dull. With the Algorithm help, fake news appear more often on Facebook browser page.

  • • Complaints were made that Facebook is what cause Trump to win the election.

  • • Russia government was creating fake news to affect the election.

  • • Counties like Philippines use Facebook to spread false information to get people to elect people to their government.

  • • High position official are using Facebook to attack other competition.

  • • Misinformation/bad context are being used to heighten people negative emotion (such as anger).

  • • Facebook decide to hire international worker to get information about Facebook influence in other countries.

  • • Cambridge Analytica got access to Facebook user Data. Which mean privacy have now been violated by third party.

  • • 2018, Facebook will renew its business model and priority as well as their mission.

  • • Facebook create a team to monitor fake story and delete false account.
