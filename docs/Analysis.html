Look Alike Analysis

The Model to Find Super User:

  • 1. Start with an Advertiser running a campaign on his/her products or services.

  • 2. Need to identify super users from the normal user.

  • 3. Super user are potential loyal customers.

  • 4. Normal user does less transaction.

  • 5. Get a huge number of computer IDs/ online fingerprinting.

  • 6. Create a feature vector: A variable/behavior that separates super user and normal user.

  • 7. Basically, setting behaviors into a positive or negative group.

  • 8. Look Alike Models: act as a tree, as two new branches are created for each vector.

  • 9. They are basically grouping customer base on behaviors.

  • 10. For instance, people who watch football and are male are more likely to buy your product than females who watch football.

  • 11. They can get information on super user preference, such as which device they tend to use to buy the product.

  • 12. They use the data to compare people's behavior with others.

  • 13. They can find variables that prevent you from buying the product.

  • 14. The whole model is basically used to maximize the amount of money a product/services can produce.
