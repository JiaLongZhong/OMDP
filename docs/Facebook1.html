Facebook Dilemma Part 1

The Rise of Facebook:

  • • Mark Zuckerberg CEO of Facebook.

  • • Facebook goal is to give people the power to share. To make the world more open and connected.

  • • Facebook is one of the first platform for people to meet online.

  • • Due to this Facebook is growing at an exponential pace.

  • • News Feed: Algorithm used to show people what they want to see.

  • • Like Button: created to engage more people with the webpage and collect users’ personal data.

  • • All these data tell Facebook people behavior, habit, and who they are connected to.

  • • No law was created that prevent companies from being sue for what posted on site.

  • • Facebook create ground rule for violent and unappropriated context.

  • • Facebook didn’t know their own potential.

  • • Example of this: Is a riot/revolution design using Facebook to force the leader of Egypt to step down.

  • • Facebook get people together. Which can be a good and bad thing.

  • • Because of Facebook Algorithm in News Feed, people with opposite view start to clash with each other non-stop.

  • • People are taking advantage of Facebook Algorithm to spread fake news.

  • • Facebook HQ have no idea how to stop this fake news. Since they can’t tell every fake news from real.

  • • Fake accounts were being created in Facebook.

  • • Facebook companies is understaffed and can’t handle all these problems.

  • • Facebook financially stop growing, so they decided to sell marketable data to data-broker companies.

  • • Facebook start invading people privacy without the people knowing.
